The Imperium Briefcase is the definitive statement of power and refinement. Crafted from a single flawless Nile crocodile skin, selected for its uniform scale pattern and rich matte finish, this briefcase requires over 120 hours of meticulous handwork by a single master artisan in our Istanbul atelier.

  • Genuine matte Nile crocodile leather (CITES certified)
  • Ruthenium-plated solid brass lock and hinges
  • Hand-stitched using waxed linen thread
  • Suede leather interior with padded laptop compartment
  • Two internal document pockets and card slots
  • Detachable shoulder strap in matching leather
  • Hand-numbered identification plate
  • Dimensions: 42cm × 30cm × 12cm
  • Weight: 2.4 kg

Each Imperium Briefcase is made to order. Please allow 4–6 weeks for our artisans to complete your piece.

  • Complimentary worldwide shipping via armored courier
  • Fully insured during transit
  • White-glove delivery with personal presentation
  • Discreet packaging with SAN DUKHAR signature box

Your SAN DUKHAR creation is designed to last for generations with proper care.

  • Complimentary cleaning and conditioning for the first 5 years
  • Lifetime repair service at a preferential rate
  • Store in the provided dust bag away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid contact with water, alcohol, and abrasive surfaces
  • Certificate of Authenticity included
